About PayPoint Nepal

Paypoint Nepal Pvt. Ltd. is a licensed fintech company in Nepal that provides online and offline electronic utility bill payment services. It operates as an intermediary payment aggregator, allowing users to settle various service bills through physical kiosks, authorized dealer networks, and digital platforms.
